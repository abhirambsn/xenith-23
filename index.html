About

In the last few years, we have come to terms with the fact that there can be nothing in real life which isn't possible and there is nothing which we can Ctrl+ delete from the existence of our history. Good, bad, endearing - all sorts of memories within our heart, we always tend to look forward to what is yet to come with infinite dreams and aspirations along with a ray of hope to go beyond the "zenith" of the knowns and unknowns and discover new forms with our creative and curious minds.With this hope and motto in our thoughts, IEEE Student Branch JIIT is thrilled to announce the second online edition of our most anticipated and largely awaited two days annual fest ~ "XENITH-23" , with the theme being "Beyond The Apex", where we explore the young minds and promote them to think beyond the reasonable. ..read more
